Support

We're here to help. Find answers below or contact us directly.

How do I generate a course?

Tap the + button on the home screen. You can upload a PDF, paste a YouTube link, or simply type a topic. Loqlearn will generate a full course with lessons, flashcards, quizzes, and diagrams automatically.

Why are my apps locked?

Loqlearn uses iOS Screen Time to lock other apps on your device until you complete your daily lesson. This is a feature, not a bug — it keeps you focused. Once you finish your lesson, your apps unlock automatically.

How do I unlock my apps?

Open Loqlearn and complete your daily lesson. Once you finish the quiz or mark the lesson as done, your apps will be unlocked for the rest of the day.

How do I turn off app blocking?

Go to Settings inside the app and disable the app blocking toggle. You can also remove Loqlearn's Screen Time permissions from your iPhone's Settings → Screen Time.

My course generation failed. What do I do?

Make sure your PDF is under 20 MB and is text-based (not a scanned image). If using a topic, try being more specific. If the problem persists, please contact us with the topic or file you used.

How do I delete a course?

On the home screen, swipe left on the course you want to delete and tap Delete.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store → tap your profile icon → Subscriptions → find Loqlearn → tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Can't find what you're looking for? We usually reply within 24 hours.
